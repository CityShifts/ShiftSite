MTH Modified is in memory of a beloved car enthusiast and team member, Martine Hearn. Martine lost her life January 2023. The team here at Revolution wanted to do something in her memory and to bring everyone together and share Martine's passion for cars. MTH Modified Show is a not for profit event. Any profits are donated to Winston's Wish - Bereavement Charity
